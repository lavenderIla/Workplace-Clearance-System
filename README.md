# Workplace-Clearance-System
This document explains the Workplace Clearance System implemented in C. The system manages staff and their clearance levels, and processes clearance requests.

Background Information about the Project
Mambo Leo Company requires a system to manage their staff and the associated clearance levels. Clearance levels are crucial for accessing various company resources and data. A digital system ensures that the staff management process is efficient, accurate, and easy to maintain.

The objectives of developing this system include:
- Providing a digital platform for adding, removing, and viewing staff details.
- Allowing staff members to submit clearance requests digitally.
- Enabling easy processing and tracking of clearance requests.
- Reducing administrative overhead and minimizing errors associated with manual processes.

To further improve the system, the following recommendations are proposed:
- Implementing a more robust authentication and authorization mechanism.
- Adding more detailed logging and audit trails for better tracking of system usage.
- Extending the system to support more complex clearance workflows and approval hierarchies.
